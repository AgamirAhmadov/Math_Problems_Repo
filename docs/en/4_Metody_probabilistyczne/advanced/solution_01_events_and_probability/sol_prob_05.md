## Task 5

### Useful Definitions and Formulas

**Complement of an event**

The complement of event $A$ means that event $A$ **does not occur**.

$$
P(A') = 1 - P(A)
$$

---

**Addition rule for probabilities**

For any two events $A$ and $B$:

$$
P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B)
$$

From this formula we can also compute the intersection:

$$
P(A \cap B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cup B)
$$

---

**De Morgan’s Law**

The probability that **neither event occurs** is equal to the complement of their union:

$$
A' \cap B' = (A \cup B)'
$$

Therefore,

$$
P(A' \cap B') = 1 - P(A \cup B)
$$

## Given Data

Event $A$:

$$
A = \langle 125, 250 \rangle
$$

$$
P(A) = 0.6
$$

Event $B$:

$$
B = (200, 300\rangle
$$

$$
P(B) = 0.7
$$

Union probability:

$$
P(A \cup B) = 0.8
$$

---

# 1. Complementary event $P(A')$

Using the complement formula:

$$
P(A') = 1 - P(A)
$$

Substitute the value:

$$
P(A') = 1 - 0.6
$$

$$
P(A') = 0.4
$$

---

# 2. Intersection $P(A \cap B)$

Using the addition rule:

$$
P(A \cap B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cup B)
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
P(A \cap B) = 0.6 + 0.7 - 0.8
$$

$$
P(A \cap B) = 0.5
$$

---

# 3. Probability $P(A' \cap B')$

Using De Morgan’s law:

$$
P(A' \cap B') = 1 - P(A \cup B)
$$

Substitute the value:

$$
P(A' \cap B') = 1 - 0.8
$$

$$
P(A' \cap B') = 0.2
$$

---

# Final Results

$$
P(A') = 0.4
$$

$$
P(A \cap B) = 0.5
$$

$$
P(A' \cap B') = 0.2
$$
